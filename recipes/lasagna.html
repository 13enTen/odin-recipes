Lasagna


Dish Description

Lasagna is a mouthwatering and comforting Italian dish that is as visually appealing as it is delicious. Layered with love and precision, this culinary masterpiece consists of wide, flat sheets of tender pasta, generously spread with a rich and savory tomato-based meat sauce. Each layer is interspersed with creamy béchamel sauce and generously sprinkled with a tantalizing blend of melted mozzarella, Parmesan, and sometimes ricotta cheese.

The harmonious marriage of flavors creates a symphony of taste that is both hearty and indulgent. Baked to golden perfection, the lasagna emerges from the oven with a bubbly, aromatic allure that captivates all who are fortunate enough to taste its complex and satisfying flavors. Whether served as a family favorite or in an elegant dining setting, lasagna remains a timeless classic that brings joy and comfort with every luscious bite.

Ingredients

  • 6 lasagna noodles
  • 1 jar (24 ounces/680g) marinara sauce
  • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup (100g) grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a baking dish (8x8-inch or 9x9-inch) lightly.

2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

3. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce at the bottom of the prepared baking dish.

4. Place three lasagna noodles over the sauce.

5. Sprinkle half of the shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the noodles.

6. Add another layer of marinara sauce over the cheese.

7. Repeat the layering with the remaining three lasagna noodles and cheeses.

8. Finish with a final layer of marinara sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese on top.

9. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.

10. Bake the lasagna in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and lightly browned.

11. Let the lasagna rest for a few minutes before serving.
